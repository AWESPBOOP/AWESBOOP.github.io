Mass and Weight whats the difference?

Mass

Mass is the amount of matter in an object. Mass can be measured with a triple beam balance scale.

Weight

Weight is the force of gravity acting on an object. Weight can be measured with a spring scale.

An example

If your on the moon your mass stays the same however you would get lighter. This is because mass always stays the same, but gravity can change.

Units of measurement

  • Weight: newtons
  • Mass: Grams
  • Volume: Ml
  • Density: Grams per cm or mil
